Habitat for Reindeer

Where on earth is a reindeer found?

Cold places with lots of grasses such as tundra, mountainous, and woodlands area.

What natural surroundings does the animal need to thrive?

Cold areas, grasses, leaves, twigs, lichen for food

Where does the animal make its home?

North America, Northern Europe, Siberia

Where does it sleep?

On the ground
